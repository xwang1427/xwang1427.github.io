About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Northeastern University, starting in 2024. I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology in December 2023, supervised by Prof. Guohua Wang. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and Bioinformatics.
